Eligibility checklist

Alignment

Clear problem, credible plan, and outcomes that directly benefit a defined community.

Capacity

Compliance

Ability to receive funds and report progress; ethical and legal standards upheld.

Frequently asked questions

Who can apply?

Individuals, teams, and registered organizations based in Nigeria or serving Nigerian communities.

How are grants disbursed?

Funds are released by milestones with light reporting to keep momentum and accountability.

Can I re-apply if rejected?

Yes. We share feedback where possible so you can refine and submit again in the next cycle.
